Sudoku Puzzle

Rules of the Game

  • Objective: Fill each row, column, and 3x3 subgrid with numbers 1–9, ensuring each number appears only once.
  • Hint: A well-balanced puzzle will have a unique solution, but not too many clues.
  • Difficulty: Varies by the number of initial clues and their placement. The fewer clues, the harder the puzzle!

Fun Facts

  • Did you know there are 6.67 quintillion possible Sudoku grids?
  • Sudoku puzzles are believed to improve logic, concentration, and even math skills!
  • Originally, Sudoku was published as a number puzzle under the name "Number Place" in 1979.
